Research Interests

An overview of my master's research focus and areas of interest

During my Master's in Technical and Professional Communication at Auburn, my research has focused on understanding how language, communication, and accessibility intersect in educational and professional settings. I am particularly interested in:

  • Language acquisition in children and adult learners
  • Multimodal communication in digital environments
  • Technical communication strategies for rural or agricultural communities
  • Language ideologies in educational or workplace contexts
  • Accessibility and Universal Design for Learning (UDL) in language instruction

Through my projects, I have explored the application of technical communication principles to real-world scenarios, including curriculum design, instructional materials, and media accessibility. My goal is to contribute to research and practice that makes communication more inclusive and effective across diverse communities.
